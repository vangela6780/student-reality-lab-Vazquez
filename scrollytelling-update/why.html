Why This Story Format Fits the Midterm

This page is intentionally calmer than the homepage: a standard reading flow with the same design system and structural clarity.

1. Better Pacing for Complex Claims

War-cost arguments include economics, casualties, and policy context. Scrollytelling helps separate those layers so your audience can process each step before moving on.

2. Clearer Evidence Boundaries

Separating context, evidence, interpretation, and action makes it easier to avoid overclaiming and to communicate limits transparently.

3. Stronger Classroom Presentation Flow

The final call-to-action stage turns your research into discussion, helping classmates move from information to informed judgment.
